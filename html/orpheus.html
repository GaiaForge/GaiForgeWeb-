Orpheus

Advanced Ecosystem Audio Control System

Solar-Powered. Autonomous. Built for Wildlife Research.

Overview

Orpheus is a sophisticated environmental audio control system designed for ecosystem management, habitat restoration, wildlife research, and various human-focused applications. Available in Basic and Pro versions, this state-of-the-art platform enables precise control over sound playback based on astronomical events, time schedules, and seasonal patterns—all while operating autonomously on solar power for months at a time.

4 Intelligent Playback Modes

Orpheus Basic and Pro both feature four powerful playback modes designed for research-grade precision:

Manual Playback

Direct audio control with Play, Pause, and Stop functionality. Perfect for on-demand playback during field observations or controlled experiments.

Interval Playback

Schedule audio at precise time windows throughout the day. Set specific intervals for targeted playback aligned with your research protocol.

Astral Playback

Sun-synchronized scheduling that automatically adjusts to sunrise and sunset times. Ideal for studying crepuscular species and dawn/dusk behaviors.

Seasonal Playback

Natural season-based scheduling (Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter) with support for custom date ranges. Perfect for migration studies and breeding seasons.

Both versions share core playback capabilities, with the Pro adding advanced monitoring and recording features for comprehensive research deployments.

Key Features

  • Astronomical-Based Playback: Schedule audio relative to sunrise and sunset, automatically adjusting to seasonal light changes at any location worldwide
  • Seasonal Programming: Define custom seasons for migration studies or use natural seasons with specific audio schedules for each
  • Location-Aware Scheduling: Latitude/longitude configuration ensures accurate sun calculations for any deployment location on Earth
  • Solar Power Integration: Built-in power management with ACS37800 sensor for real-time solar monitoring and deep sleep capabilities
  • Companion App Control: Configure and monitor remotely via Bluetooth with the Orpheus mobile app
  • Environmental Monitoring (Pro): BME280 sensor tracks temperature, humidity, and barometric pressure with data logging
  • High-Quality Recording (Pro): Capture animal responses with configurable bit depth (16/24/32-bit float) and sample rates
  • Comprehensive Logging (Pro): Detailed playback analytics with timestamps and location data for research documentation

Technical Specifications

  • Processor: Raspberry Pi / DietPi compatible SBC with custom hardware integration
  • Power: Solar-powered with ESP32-based power management and EEPROM wake scheduling
  • Current Draw: Optimized to ~50mA (67% reduction from initial design)
  • Sensors (Pro): BME280 (environmental), ACS37800 (power monitoring)
  • Audio Output: High-quality amplified speaker system
  • Recording (Pro): USB audio device support, 16/24/32-bit float, configurable sample rates
  • Connectivity: Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) for remote app control
  • Display: Touchscreen interface with real-time status and controls
  • Storage: Expandable storage for large audio libraries
  • Enclosure: Weatherproof design for field deployment
  • GPIO: Integration for external equipment control (relays, triggers)
