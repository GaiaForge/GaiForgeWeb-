SprigRig

Modular Controlled Environment Agriculture System

Smart. Adaptable. Empowering plant growth anywhere.

Overview

SprigRig is an adaptable controlled environment agriculture system designed for both home gardeners and professional growers. This versatile platform can be configured for various growing methods including hydroponics, drip irrigation, greenhouse control, and aeroponics, making it suitable for a wide range of applications from small-scale indoor growing to commercial operations.

Key Features

  • Modular Design: Easily expand or reconfigure your system as your needs change, with plug-and-play components
  • Smart Irrigation Control: Precision watering based on soil moisture levels, time schedules, and plant-specific needs
  • Environmental Monitoring: Track temperature, humidity, light levels, and soil conditions in real-time
  • Data Logging & Analysis: Store historical data to track growth patterns and optimize growing conditions over time
  • Mobile App Control: Monitor and adjust your system remotely with intuitive mobile interface
  • Energy-Efficient Operation: Smart power management with solar integration options for sustainable growing
  • Expandable Sensor Network: Add specialized sensors for pH, EC, CO2, and other parameters as needed
  • Open API: Integrate with third-party systems and contribute to our growing developer community
